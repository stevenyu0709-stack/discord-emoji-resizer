How to resize an image for a Twitch emote

  1. Click the upload box above (or drag your image onto it).
  2. The tool instantly resizes it to 112×112 pixels — the size Twitch uses for emotes.
  3. It automatically compresses the file to stay under Twitch's upload limit.
  4. Click Download Emote and upload it in your Twitch creator dashboard.

What size are Twitch emotes?

Twitch asks creators to upload emotes at 112×112 pixels. Twitch then automatically generates the smaller 56×56 and 28×28 versions used in chat. Upload at 112×112 for the best quality.
